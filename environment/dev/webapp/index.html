    Advertising

    The action of calling public attention to an idea, good, or service through paid announcements by an identified sponsor.

    • SEM “Search Engine Marketing”

      • Usage of paid ad campaigns based on keywords to connect to users actively searching for.

    • PPC “Pay Per Click Ads”

      • A pattern of advertising allows marketers to pay only when their advertisement is clicked by an online user.

    • PPM “Pay Per Impression Ads”

      • It’s a digital advertising model in which the advertiser pays an amount each time a user clicks on one of their ads to visit their website.

    • SMM “Social Media Marketing”

      • Utilizing social media websites and social networks to market your product or service.

    • SMS Campaigns

      • A form of marketing that helps you to interact with your customers via text messages over their phones.

    • GDN “Google Display Network Ads”

      • Assist you to attain a target audience while they’re browsing their favorite websites, watching YouTube videos, checking their Gmail account, or even using mobile apps.

    • Print Ads

      • A form of advertising uses printed media like newspapers and magazines to reach prospects and customers.

    • Guerilla Ads

      • A strategy that focuses on yielding maximum results with low-cost unconventional marketing tactics.

    • TV Ads

      • All about sending specific messages, promotional or media content, to potential customers through a span of TV programming.

    • Offline Ads

      • Using traditional media such as outdoor and billboards advertising, print media, TV and radio advertisements to create awareness of a specific.
